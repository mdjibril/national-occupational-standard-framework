NATIONAL SKILLS QUALIFICATION // PAINTING AND DECORATION // LEVEL 1 - 3
Expectations
The candidate or learner on the job needs to demonstrate the following:
1. Demonstrate Procedures of evacuation of workers during emergencies.
2. Demonstrate Health, safety and Environment accident reporting procedures.
3. Conduct Conflict Resolution at the work place.
4. Carry out Procedures for handling, stacking and storage of materials in accordance with
specifications.
5. Identify Hazards related to Painting and decorating works.
6. Conduct Standard housekeeping procedures at work place.
7. Identify types of materials for preparation of surfaces for painting and decoration
8. Carry out Mixing and matching of different colors in painting and decoration
9. Mix proportions of paints for painting and decoration
11. Use Tools and equipment for erecting and dismantling scaffolds, work platforms and
ladders.
12. Identify Quality and function of paints and its base type.
13. Demonstrate different methods of paint application.
14. Cast panels, screed surfaces, install wallpaper and wall panels.
